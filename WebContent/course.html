    C programming

    No prior programming experience is necessary! Our C app will supply you with everything you need to create and compile your own programs. We guarantee that learning with Athena is fast, effective, and fun. This app will show you how easy it is to become a programmer. Learn C includes over 45 lessons that cover basic concepts, data types, arrays, pointers, conditional statements, loops, functions, structures, unions, and much more. Unlock new levels, take quizzes, collect points, and compete with peers from around the world. Most importantly, learn while having fun, because being engaged when learning makes all difference in the world.
